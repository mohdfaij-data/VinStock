Our Mission

One platform. Every metric
that matters.

Serious stock research requires jumping between too many tools — financial data sites, screeners, valuation calculators, and charting platforms. VinStock was built to eliminate that friction.

By consolidating financial statements, valuation ratios, profitability metrics, growth analysis and screening tools into a single cohesive interface, investors can move from idea to conviction faster and with more confidence.

The goal is simple: make the kind of rigorous, data-driven analysis that was once reserved for professionals accessible to every investor.

Bridge the gap between financial analysis and technology — so that data-driven investing is available to everyone.

Platform Features

Everything you need to evaluate
any business.

Six core modules designed to take you from initial screening to deep due diligence without ever leaving the platform.

Stock Analysis

Evaluate companies using key financial metrics and ratios — PE, PB, ROE, ROCE and more — in a single structured view.

Financial Statements

Visualize revenue, net profit, assets, liabilities and cash flow across multiple years to spot real trends.

Watchlists

Build and organize a list of companies you're researching. Monitor them in one place without losing context.

Stock Screener

Filter thousands of stocks by custom criteria — valuations, growth rates, profitability — to surface the right opportunities.

Peer Comparison

Benchmark any company side by side against its direct competitors within the same sector and industry.

Growth Analysis

Track revenue CAGR, EPS growth, and profit margins over time to separate genuinely compounding businesses.
